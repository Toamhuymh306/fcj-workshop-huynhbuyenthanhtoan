**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS fundamentals, IAM, CLI, S3, and Cognito](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Lambda and API Gateway fundamentals](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Secure upload flow with Pre-signed URL](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [AI model preparation and optimization](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [AI containerization and Amazon ECR](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Deploy AI container on AWS Lambda](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Event-driven architecture with Amazon SQS](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Persist inference results with DynamoDB](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Storage cost optimization with S3 Lifecycle](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [End-to-end system integration testing](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Monitoring and alerting with CloudWatch and SNS](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Final report, cleanup, and project handover](1.12-week12/)
