**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period). However, the KTs team optimized the process and completed the project over **8 weeks** (from May 11, 2026, to July 12, 2026) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS fundamentals, IAM, S3, and Cognito](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [AI data processing (PlantVillage Dataset)](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [AI model training and optimization (CNN PyTorch)](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [AI containerization and Amazon ECR](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Serverless Backend deployment (AWS Lambda & DynamoDB)](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [API Gateway security and secure upload flow](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Frontend web interface development](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [End-to-end system testing, final demo, and report](1.8-week8/)
