# FCAJ Community Day

## Event Information

- **Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day
- **Time:** 09:00 AM - 12:00 PM, Saturday, May 23, 2026
- **Location:** Floor 26, Bitexco Financial Tower, Ho Chi Minh City
- **Role:** Attendee

## Main Content

This was a deep-dive community event focusing on AWS solutions and technology trends. Key sessions included:

- **Platform Engineering and AI Context:** The critical role of providing context for AI and the industry shift toward platform engineering.
- **Amazon CloudFront:** Optimizing CDN cost and security with the BlackRatePricing mechanism.
- **Hackathon Experience:** A 36-hour journey of building the UTMorpho project from scratch at LotusHacks.
- **LLM Optimization:** Understanding non-determinism in LLMs even when Temperature is set to 0.
- **Enterprise Multi-Agent System:** Designing enterprise-grade multi-agent architectures for startup credit scoring with strong focus on security and risk governance.

## Key Learnings and Personal Contributions

- **Enterprise AI Mindset:** I recognized a major gap between building a proof of concept and deploying AI into production. Security constraints, risk management (for example Prompt Injection mitigation), and attack-surface control are more critical than simply making the model generate answers.
- **Technical LLM Insights:** The session from Duc Dao clarified why AI output can be inconsistent. Understanding floating-point precision effects and cloud-provider inference optimizations helps me design better prompting strategies and system configurations (such as self-hosting or Station Mode).
- **Multi-Agent Design Thinking:** I learned the specialization principle (each agent should own one focused responsibility) and the need for environment isolation to avoid data leakage. This changed how I approach complex multi-step AI workflows.
