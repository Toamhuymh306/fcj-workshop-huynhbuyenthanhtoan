During my internship at **Amazon Web Services Viet Nam Company Limited** from **April 17 to July 30, 2026**, I had the opportunity to apply the knowledge gained at **Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HUTECH)** in a professional working environment.

I participated in the **KTS Smart Agri** project, which focused on developing an AI-powered plant leaf disease diagnosis system using a **serverless AWS architecture**. I contributed to user authentication with Amazon Cognito, direct image uploads through Amazon S3 pre-signed URLs, the asynchronous S3-SQS-Lambda inference pipeline, container deployment with Amazon ECR, input validation with Amazon Rekognition, result storage in DynamoDB, monitoring with CloudWatch, and frontend delivery through CloudFront.

Through these tasks, I strengthened my skills in **Python, PyTorch, Docker, serverless architecture, cloud security, testing, and AWS operations**. I also improved my teamwork, task management, technical documentation, and ability to troubleshoot an end-to-end system.

In terms of work attitude, I consistently strived to complete assigned tasks to the best of my ability, complied with company regulations, and actively collaborated with colleagues to increase team productivity.
